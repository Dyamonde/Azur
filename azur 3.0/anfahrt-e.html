How to reach us?


From Berliner Ring (A10), Exit No.4, Hellersdorf-Straußberg, take the B1 to Berlin-Mitte.
After crossing the Berlin City limits take a right at the fifth Traffic Light, into Myslowitzer Straße.
400 Yards down, take a left into Ferdinandstraße.

or

Coming from Berlin, take the B1 to Frankfurt/Oder.
In Berlin Kaulsdorf take a left into Myslowitzer Straße.
400 Yards down, take another left into Ferdinandstraße.

For S-Bahn, you have to take the Line 5 to Strausberg Nord and exit at Kaulsdorf Station.
