About us:

our Hotel was built 1993 in Berlin Kaulsdorf. Kaulsdorf is on the outer reaches of Berlin, far away from the hassle of the main city but still close enough to get there in a flash.


It takes only 20 minutes of easy and comfortable train travel and the nearest station is just a five minute walk away from the hotel. A train leaves every ten minutes, so you should´nt have trouble to get one without having to wait for ages.


And while you explore the wonders of our beautiful capital your car waits safely on our parking lot - free of charge, of course.
